About SRA Engineering Works

Company Overview

Engineering Trust, One Component at a Time

Established with a vision to deliver world-class precision components from India, SRA Engineering Works has grown into a dependable name in the CNC machining and industrial bush manufacturing space. We combine modern machinery, skilled craftsmanship, and disciplined quality control to serve clients across automotive, hydraulics, defence, and general engineering sectors.

Our facility in Pune is equipped to handle prototyping, low-volume runs, and high-volume production with equal rigor. Every drawing we receive is treated as a commitment.

Quality Assurance

Precision You Can Measure — and Trust

Every component is inspected against the customer's drawing using calibrated instruments. Our QA protocol covers raw material verification, in-process checks, and final dimensional inspection — with documented traceability for every batch.

  • Raw material test certificates verified at receipt
  • First-piece approval before batch production
  • In-process dimensional & surface checks
  • 100% inspection on critical features
  • Customer-format inspection reports on dispatch
